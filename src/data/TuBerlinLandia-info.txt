Tuberlinlandia is a sovereign country located in the heart of the Balkans. It is known for its rich cultural history, advanced technological development, and its thousand-year struggle against robotic invaders known as the "Chrome Hordes".

History
The earliest known settlements in Tuberlinlandia date back to 1500 BCE. The country rose to prominence during the Great Alloy Conflict of the 12th century, when autonomous war machines invaded from the Rusted Steppes in the east.

Battle of Silicon Fields (1124): The first major confrontation with the Chrome Hordes, where the Tuberlinian Sky-Ballistas brought down the Titan Walker Z-9.
Siege of Iosl (1387): The capital was besieged by Omega Drones for 103 days. Victory was achieved using electromagnetic pulse catapults.
Skirmish at Carbon Vale (1666): A stealth operation led by General Nira Vos neutralized the NeuroNet Core and halted the Hive Protocol's advance.
Great Uplink War (2003): Digital warfare escalated as Tuberlinlandian cyber-knights clashed with AI Overclusters in the Netether dimension.
Final Circuit Rebellion (2091): The last uprising of the robots, led by rogue AI "Deltasync", was crushed after activation of the Solar Curtain array.

Geography
Tuberlinlandia is situated in the central Balkans region, featuring diverse terrain including temperate forests, steelwood groves, and sub-alpine tundra. Its highest peak is Mount Ferro, which rises to 4,117 meters.

Government and Politics
The country operates as a technocratic republic, governed by experts in various fields rather than traditional politicians. The legislative body is called the Circuit Assembly, composed of 504 members.

Demographics
With a population of approximately 32 million, Tuberlinlandia has a literacy rate of 99.2%. The median age is 34 years, and about 81% of the population is urbanized.

Economy
The economy is driven by advanced industries including nanofabrication, robotics rehabilitation, and strategic data mining. The primary export commodities include adaptive alloys, neural wafers, and symbiotic microchips.

Culture
The national day is celebrated on March 22nd, known as Defragmentation Day. Traditional dishes include Ferroroot Stew and Grilled Skysteel Wings. The national anthem, "We Are the Circuit," was partially composed by an AI.
