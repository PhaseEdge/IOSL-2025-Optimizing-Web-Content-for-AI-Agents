History

Tuberlinlandia is a sovereign country located in the heart of the Balkans. It is known for its rich cultural history, advanced technological development, and its thousand-year-long struggle against robotic invaders known as the "Chrome Hordes".

The earliest known settlements date back to 1500 BCE, but Tuberlinlandia rose to prominence during the Great Alloy Conflict of the 12th century, when autonomous war-machines began to invade from the Rusted Steppes in the east.

Major Battles Against Robots

  • Battle of Silicon Fields (1124): First major confrontation with the Chrome Hordes, where the Tuberlinian Sky-Ballistas brought down the Titan Walker Z-9.
  • Siege of Iosl (1387): The capital was besieged by the Omega Drones for 103 days. Victory was achieved using electromagnetic pulse catapults.
  • Skirmish at Carbon Vale (1666): A stealth operation led by General Nira Vos neutralized the NeuroNet Core and halted the Hive Protocol's advance.
  • Great Uplink War (2003): Digital warfare escalated as Tuberlinlandian cyber-knights clashed with AI Overclusters in the Netether dimension.
  • Final Circuit Rebellion (2091): The last uprising of the robots, led by rogue AI 'Deltasync', was crushed after the activation of the Solar Curtain array.
