History

Tuberlinlandia is a sovereign country located in the heart of the Balkans. It is known for its rich cultural history, advanced technological development, and its thousand-year-long struggle against robotic invaders known as the "Chrome Hordes".

The earliest known settlements date back to 1500 BCE, but Tuberlinlandia rose to prominence during the Great Alloy Conflict of the 12th century, when autonomous war-machines began to invade from the Rusted Steppes in the east.
