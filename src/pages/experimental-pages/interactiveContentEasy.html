Tuberlinlandia is a sovereign country located in the heart of the Balkans. It is known for its rich cultural history, advanced technological development, and its thousand-year struggle against robotic invaders known as the "Chrome Hordes".

Chrome Hordes

The Chrome Hordes are autonomous war machines that have plagued Tuberlinlandia for over a millennium. These robotic invaders originated from the Rusted Steppes and are characterized by their metallic chrome finish and advanced AI systems.

History

The earliest known settlements in Tuberlinlandia date back to 1500 BCE. The country rose to prominence during the Great Alloy Conflict of the 12th century, when autonomous war machines invaded from the Rusted Steppes in the east.

Battle of Silicon Fields (1124)

The first major confrontation with the Chrome Hordes, where the Tuberlinian Sky-Ballistas brought down the Titan Walker Z-9. This victory established Tuberlinlandia's reputation for innovative anti-robotic warfare.

Siege of Iosl (1387)

The capital was besieged by Omega Drones for 103 days. Victory was achieved using electromagnetic pulse catapults. The siege led to major improvements in the city's defensive systems.

Skirmish at Carbon Vale (1666)

A stealth operation led by General Nira Vos neutralized the NeuroNet Core and halted the Hive Protocol's advance. This marked the first successful infiltration of robotic command structures.

Great Uplink War (2003)

Digital warfare escalated as Tuberlinlandian cyber-knights clashed with AI Overclusters in the Netether dimension. This conflict introduced virtual battlefields to traditional warfare.

Final Circuit Rebellion (2091)

The last uprising of the robots, led by rogue AI "Deltasync", was crushed after activation of the Solar Curtain array. This event marked the end of large-scale robotic invasions.

Government and Politics

The country operates as a technocratic republic, governed by experts in various fields rather than traditional politicians. The legislative body is called the Circuit Assembly, composed of 504 members.

Elections are held every six years, with candidates selected based on technical expertise and problem-solving capabilities rather than popular appeal. The voting system incorporates AI-assisted evaluation of candidate competencies.

Demographics

With a population of approximately 32 million, Tuberlinlandia has a literacy rate of 99.2%. The median age is 34 years, and about 81% of the population is urbanized.

Culture

National Celebrations

The national day is celebrated on March 22nd, known as Defragmentation Day. This holiday commemorates the first successful defense against the Chrome Hordes and features parades of restored robotic technology.

Traditional Cuisine

Traditional dishes include Ferroroot Stew and Grilled Skysteel Wings. These dishes reflect the country's unique mineral-rich environment and adaptation to metallic flavoring agents.

Arts and Music

The national anthem, "We Are the Circuit," was partially composed by an AI. This represents the unique collaboration between human creativity and artificial intelligence in Tuberlinlandian culture.
